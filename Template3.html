Welcome to BOOM COMICS!

About Us:

We are an enthusiastic team aiming to provide a wide range of comics in this day and age of technology and hope to incite the curiosity and excitement of the readers of Gen-Z.

The Boom Comics is a store containing comics from over 100 publishers and comic labels, covering wide range of genres of fiction from Sci-Fi to Mythological to Misclleneous.

We have a large selection of graphic novels, role-playing games, trading cards, collectible card games, and miniatures. We look forward to seeing you in our store to explore more.

With our recent opening, we aim to set-up our outlets across 10 citites within the next 5 years providing that our brand and store get positive reviews and support from the viewers.

"We don't just sell comics"

In addition to our comprehensive selection of new and classic comic books, we pride ourselves on providing our customers with an array of comic related memorabilia. We have a large selection of graphic novels, role-playing games, trading cards, collectible card games, and miniatures. We look forward to seeing you in our store and hope that you can come visit us soon!

New Comics arrive every week on Wednesday morning. We open at 9:00am and the week’s new issues are ready to be sold at that time. We have a gigantic array of graphic novels on display with new ones and restocks arriving weekly.

The Boom Comics Store offers a wide variety of Collectibles. We also have a full selection of Wiz Kids games and fun tournament play is usually held on special occasions.

CCG such as the ever popular “Magic the Gathering”, VS (Marvel & DC), Vanguard, Pokeman and Yu-gi-oh are a constant seller at the Comic Store.
